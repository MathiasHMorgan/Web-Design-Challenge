Introduction

This projects purpose was to conduct weather change analysis depending on the latitude and distance from the equator. Data from major cities was collected and analysed.

The data was analysed for a correlation between the latitude distance from the equator on both hemispheres. Analysis was performed considering the following factors: temperature, cloudiness, wind speed, and humidity.
